Hello and welcome to GlucOracle,
your personal glucose forecasting assistant!

GlucOracle is a research project at Columbia University and you are now participating in a research study. To learn more about GlucOracle, check out our FAQs below.

    Website FAQ Page    

Now that you have joined the GlucOracle family, let’s go ahead and get started. Start logging your meals and blood glucose levels before and after each meal. For details about how to use GlucOracle, click here.

GlucOracle uses a sophisticated computational model to learn how your body processes meals and controls blood glucose. The more you log, the better the forecasts will be—personalized forecasts only become available after you’ve recorded at least 20 meals and 50 blood glucose readings.

We realize that recording 20 meals takes time and commitment. To make it more interesting, after you record your first 9 meals, for each new meal you will receive its nutritional assessment from a crowdsourcing engine. To find out more about this crowdsourcing mechanism, click here.

For questions or comments, please submit a comment on our website or email us at support@GlucOracle.com.

Thank you for joining us and let’s get started!
