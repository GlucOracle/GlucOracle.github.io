Nutritional estimation

It's hard!

we know it's hard

That is why we bring the power of collective intelligence. Our app, GlucOracle, sends your meals to Amazon Mechanical Turkers (AMT) for nutritional estimation using corwdsourcing workflow. You will receive a crowdsourced estimation of your meal, in comparison to yours, to obsreve if there is a difference. Once the crowd's solution is back, you can see the difference between your estimation and the crowd's estimation of your meal. For additional information on this process click here.
