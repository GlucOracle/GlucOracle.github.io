How to use the app

Easy Steps

Use GlucOracle to make more informed choices

forecast your blood glucose

 

Use the app to choose the right meal for you. If you are not happy with the forecast, change the meal, take another photo, and receive a new forecast. Repeat until you are happy with your outcome. Do not settle till you are happy with your results.

Nutritional estimation

It's hard!

That is why we bring the power of collective intelligence. Our app, GlucOracle, sends your meals to Amazon Mechanical Turkers (AMT) for nutritional estimation using corwdsourcing workflow PlateMate developed by our colleagues at Harvard University. Once the crowd's solution is ready, you can view it in our app and compare your own estimate with the estimate by the crowd. For additional information about PlateMate click here.
