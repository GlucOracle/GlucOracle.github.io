What we do as a research team


What is GlucOracle?


GlucOracle is a research project at the Department of Biomedical Informatics at Columbia University. The goal of this research is to develop novel technologies for diabetes self-management that use computational analysis of self-monitoring data. We design new research prototypes and conduct studies to assess whether they help individuals with diabetes improve their glycemic control. Let us know if you would like to find out more about our group!

We use the blood sugar and nutrition data you log in the GlucOracle app to train a computational model of how your endocrine system works. The green curve on the left shows GlucOracle's predicted response for today's dinner. It takes the model 7 days or 20 recorded meals to generate personalized forecasts. The more records you capture, the sooner you will start receiving forecasts.
